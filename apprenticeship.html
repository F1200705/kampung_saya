My Internship Experience at PT IndoRental (Aspiluki)

During my internship at PT IndoRental (Aspiluki), I was entrusted with two key areas: Office Administration and Graphic Design & Web Content Management. This experience gave me a practical understanding of how business processes and digital communication are implemented in the real world.

Tasks and Work Performed

Di bidang Administrasi, saya mengerjakan:

  • Digitally archived over 100 work documents, speeding up data retrieval and improving administrative efficiency.
  • Numbered important documents, such as PCs and invoices, to facilitate tracking and organization.
  • Inputted daily data into Google Sheets as the basis for operational reports.
  • Sent coordination emails to colleagues and partners for document validation.
  • Assisted with the administrative process for extending intercompany contracts.
  • Created presentation slides using PowerPoint and Google Slides for management to use in internal meetings.

In the field of Design and Web, I contribute by:

  • Designed logos, promotional posters, and product stickers using Canva and Photoshop.
  • Updated the company website's appearance and content using HTML and CSS.
  • Regularly posted news and new content to the company website with the IT team.

Benefits I Get

Through this experience, I learned to:

  • Work in a structured and efficient manner when handling documents.
  • Communicate professionally both verbally and in writing, especially through business emails.
  • Understand digital workflows, from data processing and reporting to website content management.
  • Develop technical skills in Microsoft Office, Google Workspace, HTML/CSS, and graphic design.

The Added Value I Provide to the Company

During the internship, I not only carried out the assigned tasks, but also:

  • Improve the efficiency of administrative processes by organizing archives digitally and systematically.
  • Help companies present data more professionally through presentations and visuals I create.
  • Improve the appearance of the company website to make it look neater, more modern, and more informative.
  • Take the initiative to consistently post web content, which helps maintain the company's information media presence.
